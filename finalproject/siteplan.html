Atlantic Medical Diagnostics Site Plan

Location Profile: Oron, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria

1. Site Identity & Branding

Site Name:

Atlantic Medical Diagnostics

Target Identifier Rationale:

This brand framework directly targets the medical services sector in Oron, signaling trust and operational urgency. The title establishes regional identity while emphasizing medical compliance and advanced diagnostics standards.

Proposed Domain Registration:

atlanticdiagnostics.org

2. Site Purpose

The web portal serves as a digital point-of-engagement for patients, clinics, and medical staff in Oron. It streamlines patient workflows by shifting physical queues into an online booking queue. Patients can securely browse diagnostic profiles (e.g., radiology, pathology, laboratory test panels), view accurate pricing schedules, schedule collection times, and receive clear preparation checklists prior to arriving at the medical facility.

3. Target Audience User Scenarios

These operational scenarios guide layout requirements and hierarchy for our development sprints:

  • Scenario A (Patient Intake): "I am a local resident in Oron who received a lab testing referral from my primary physician. How can I quickly check if Atlantic Medical Diagnostics offers this test online, find out what it costs, and schedule an appointment for Friday morning?"
  • Scenario B (Preparation Requirements): "I need to schedule an ultrasound and an overnight fasting blood sugar test. Where can I find the specific pre-test preparation instructions so my results remain accurate and clinically sound?"

4. Architectural Color Scheme

This layout operates exclusively within your designated color scheme, balancing corporate clinical trust with high-visibility interaction states:

High-Contrast Royal Blue (#0b3c95) Primary corporate visual identity anchor. Used for primary navigation paths, layout containers, headers, and structural text scales to satisfy strict accessibility limits.
Fire Red (#e2231a) Primary interactive accent token. Reserved for critical transactional call-to-actions, booking checkouts, and system notification indicators.
Dark Slate Neutral (#1e293b) Applied directly to layout paragraphs and fine-print text tags to maintain absolute accessibility contrast.

5. Typography & Font Mapping

Global Interface Font Set (Headings, Body text, Tables, Inputs):

Font Family: 'Roboto', sans-serif

Applied universally across all text types. Roboto provides a clean, neutral, and highly technical geometric sans-serif frame that maximizes legibility on desktop displays and mobile screen tracks alike.
